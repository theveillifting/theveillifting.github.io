Language of the Birds

The "Language of the Birds" is a mystical concept that refers to a perfect, divine, and often secret language understood by the enlightened. It appears in myths, alchemical writings, and poetry as a symbol of higher wisdom and hidden truths that can only be heard by those prepared to receive them.
