Language of the Birds

— ◆ —

The "Language of the Birds" is a mystical concept referring to a perfect, divine, and often secret language understood by the enlightened. It appears in myths, alchemical writings, and poetry as a symbol of higher wisdom and hidden truths — heard only by those prepared to receive them.
